Internationalization support comes with most distros, so it is actually not necessary to install it.

These instructions are for embedded environments where one has to have the minimal install.

Obtain the Files

  1. Put the localedef binary into /usr/sbin

  2. Obtain the appropriate i18n tree for your distro and place it in /usr/share/i18n
